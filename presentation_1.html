Project Overview

Project Goals

Moana Academy was created to bridge the gap between traditional design education and the rapidly evolving AI-powered creative landscape.

01

Democratize AI Design Education

Make advanced AI design tools accessible to everyone — from complete beginners to seasoned professionals — through structured, affordable, and mentor-guided learning paths.

02

Bridge the Industry Skills Gap

Address the critical shortage of AI-literate designers by providing comprehensive training that combines traditional design principles with cutting-edge AI workflows.

03

Enable Career Transformation

Empower career changers and aspiring designers to break into the industry with job-ready portfolios, industry connections, and confidence to compete in the AI-enhanced landscape.

04

Combat AI Anxiety in Design

Shift the narrative from "AI will replace designers" to "AI empowers designers" by teaching ethical, creative, and strategic use of AI tools that amplify human creativity.

05

Build a Learning Community

Create more than just a course — build an ecosystem of learners, mentors, and industry professionals who support each other through collaborative projects and networking.

06

Achieve Measurable Outcomes

Deliver tangible results: 89% job placement rate, average time to first income of 3 months, and 50,000+ active students globally with a 4.9/5 satisfaction rating.

Context

The Challenge

The design industry is undergoing a revolutionary transformation with AI tools. However, most designers feel overwhelmed, underprepared, or fearful of being replaced.

  The Problem

78% of designers report feeling unprepared for AI integration. Existing courses are either too expensive, too technical, or quickly become outdated as tools evolve weekly.

  Our Solution

Moana Academy provides structured, mentor-guided education that empowers designers to harness AI as a creative superpower — with continuously updated content and real-world projects.

  Key Insight

"From Overwhelmed → Empowered" — Students don't need to learn every AI tool. They need a framework for thinking creatively with AI.

Before

Confused by complex AI interfaces, expensive tools, and no clear learning path

After

Confident AI-powered designer with portfolio, skills, and job placement
