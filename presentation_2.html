01 — Context

The Challenge

From Overwhelmed → Empowered

The design industry is undergoing a revolutionary transformation with AI tools. However, most designers feel overwhelmed, underprepared, or fearful of being replaced.

Moana Academy was created to bridge this gap — providing structured, mentor-guided education that empowers designers to harness AI as a creative superpower.

😰

Before

Confused designers overwhelmed by complex AI interfaces and unclear learning paths

🚀

After

Confident creators mastering AI tools with structured guidance and real-world projects

02 — Vision

Project Goals

01

Democratize AI Education

Make advanced AI design tools accessible to everyone through structured, affordable, and mentor-guided learning paths.

02

Bridge the Skills Gap

Address the critical shortage of AI-literate designers by combining traditional principles with cutting-edge workflows.

03

Enable Career Transformation

Empower career changers and freelancers to break into the industry with job-ready portfolios and industry connections.

04

Combat AI Anxiety

Shift the narrative from "AI will replace designers" to "AI empowers designers" through ethical, strategic teaching.

05

Build Community

Create an ecosystem of learners, mentors, and professionals who support each other through collaboration and networking.

06

Deliver Measurable Impact

Achieve 89% job placement, 3-month time-to-income, and 50,000+ active students with 4.9/5 satisfaction rating.
